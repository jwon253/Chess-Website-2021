The Game

Chess is a board game Chess is a board game. With two players. The objective of the game in Chess is to checkmate the opponent’s king. With any player’s king unable to move and in check. White always first to move, players take turns alternating moving one piece at a time. Until one player’s king is in checkmate. Pieces can be obtained by capturing the opponents pieces. A piece may move to another position or may capture an opponent piece.

Strategy

A game of chess involves intelligence, there is usually strategy involved. There are many different strategies that players can use to gain an advantage over the opponent. With each piece having different movement. When combined together in a sequence of setup of pieces or the position of a piece. Creating a lead towards winning a game of Chess.
