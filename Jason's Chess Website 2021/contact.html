Ormiston Chess Club

At Ormiston Senior College, Chess Club is a great opportunity for anyone interested in Chess. For new or returning players who may want to play a few games of Chess causually. At Chess Club there is a leaderboard ranking, where players feeling more competitive can challenge other experienced players. Chess Club is on Wednesdays at 3:00pm at Papanukaute commons.
